01 The Mark

Origin Ring

An open circle marks the point of departure — where an API request begins. The unfilled centre signals potential: a location to be resolved.

Pill-Dash Route

Rounded capsule dashes trace a W-shaped journey across the canvas — the five waypoints spell the brand's initial while evoking a mapped route.

Destination Pin

A solid filled dot with a downward-pointing tail — the answer to the query; where you arrive. Silver signals resolution and precision.

06 Usage
✓ Do

Use white mark on dark backgrounds (#0C0C0C, #161616, or other dark surfaces).

✓ Do

Use the dark inverted version on white and very light neutral backgrounds.

✓ Do

Adequate clear space maintained

Always maintain clear space equal to the height of the origin ring on all sides of the mark.

✗ Don't

Don't place the logo on coloured backgrounds without explicit brand approval. The Obsidian system is black / white / silver only.

✗ Don't

Don't stretch, compress, or skew the mark. Always scale proportionally from the original SVG source files.

✗ Don't

Don't apply shadows, glows, gradients, or other effects to the mark. Use it exactly as provided.

Resolve your first location.

// GET /v1/resolve

  -H "Authorization: Bearer whr_..."
